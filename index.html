ABOUT US

Century Garden was founded in 2021 by Miss Upasana Dhekial along with her family, seeking to bring exotic and indigenous houseplant varieties among the people.

We offer nearly 100 plant varieties, both flowering plants as well as foliage, seasonal and perennial.

“No other life is as pure as the plants. It is no wonder we cannot understand them.”

Robert Black



Opening hours: Everyday from 10am to 6pm.

Address: South Khalihamari, Opposite Christ Church, Dibrugarh-786001, Assam

SOME VARIETIES THAT WE HAVE
Flowers
Foliage
Bicolor Bougainvillea

A beautiful, low, spreading flowering plant with white and soft pink blossom-like bracts.


Orange Hibiscus

Beautiful flowering plant with large, conspicuous, trumpet-shaped, orange flowers.


Red Mandevilla

A tender woody vine much beloved by gardeners in warmer climates.


Dark Purple Phalaenopsis

Tear-shaped purple flowers, easy to get reflowering and to care for.

String of Nickles

It is a vining succulent that is native to the tropical regions of Asia, India, and Australia.


Fiddle-Leaf Fig

It is a popular indoor tree featuring very large, heavily veined leaves that grow upright on a sleek trunk.


Syngonium Wendlandii

An eye catching tropical plant with emerald green leaves and a white midvein. As this plant matures, leaves become deeply lobed to the point of forming separated leaflets.


Calathea Yellow Fusion

The Yellow Fusion variety of Calathea has lovely green and Yellow marbling on the leaves with pale purple undersides. It is a wonderfully showy, decorative plant that is bound to attract attention.
